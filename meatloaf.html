Hotchkiss Meatloaf

by Christopher Hotchkiss

Ingredients

  • 3 Slices white sandwhich bread
  • 1/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 medium onion, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup Heinz 57 sauce
  • coarse salt and ground pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil or parchment paper. Place bread in food processor; pulse until fine crumbs form. Transfer to a small bowl; stir in milk. Set aside, about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Meanwhile, in a small bowl combine the ketchup and Heinz 57; in a large bowl, combine beef, pork, onion, garlic, egg, 1/4 cup of the ketchup and 57 mixture, 1 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Add bread-milk mixture, and mix very gently with a fork (do not overmix).
  3. Place meat mixture on prepared baking sheet, and form into a loaf about 9 inches long and 4 to 5 inches wide.
  4. Bake, brushing twice with the remaining ketchup-57 mixture during baking, until an instant-read thermometer registers 160°, 45 to 55 minutes. Let meatloaf rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Tasty Extras

  • Bacon
    • Before baking, lay 8 slices of bacon crosswise over the meatloaf, slightly overlapping. Using a thin metal spatula or dinner knife, tuck ends of bacon under the meatloaf.
  • Crispy Onions
    • Before baking, toss 1 halved and thinly sliced small onion with 1 teaspoon of olive oil. Sprinkle on top of meatloaf, pressing in slightly (onions will be piled quite high).
  • Cheddar
    • Before forming meatloaf, gently mix 1/2 cup finely grated heddar into meat mixture. Before baking, sprinkle another 1/2 cup cheese on top of meatloaf.
