Grandma Gordon's Banana Pudding

by Stephaine Gordon

Ingredients

  • 1 box of vanilla wafers
  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons corn starch or flour (grandma used corn starch)
  • 2 cups milk or 1 1/2 cups milk and 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 Tsp vanilla
  • a pinch of salt

Directions

If you want to create a meringue for the top, set aside the egg whites in a glass bowl after separating the yolks. Otherwise, discard. Preheat the oven to 350°F

Combine all ingredients and heat on the stove over low heat, stirring constantly, until the pudding thickens.

In an oven-safe container, put a layer of vanilla wafers on the bottom and a row along the wall. Pour about half of the pudding in, then repeat with another layer and the rest of the pudding.

If you are making meringue, whip the egg yolks until it creats peaks, then spoon it onto the top of the pudding.

Bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the top is browned.
